How to Find Your Cookie

Discover your Crumbl flavor, Zodiac sign, and personal soundscape.

Step 1: Pick Your Birthday

Use the Date Selector to input your birthday. Our "Cookie-mancer" algorithm will instantly assign a Crumbl flavor to you based on your birth date.

Step 2: Experience the Vibe

Each cookie is paired with a unique Color and Sound. Every time you change the date, the background color and audio will shift to match your cookie's energy!

Step 3: Explore Zodiac Signs

Want to see the stars? Click on any cookie image to reveal the Zodiac sign that corresponds to that specific flavor.

Pro Tip: Feeling indecisive? You can switch the date as many times as you like to see different cookies and their cosmic matches.
